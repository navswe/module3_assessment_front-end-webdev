My Neighborhood

I live in Asiatown, southwest Houston. It's evolving everyday.

Located in the International District in southwest Houston, Houston's Asiatown is an amazing neighborhood bursting with wonderful food, culture, events, and communities. You'll hear people chatting in their native Chinese, Vietnamese, or Korean languages on the streets. When you're here, it's like being transported to little Asia.

Now, let's talk about the food. Chinese, Vietnamese, Japanese Korean, Thai, Laotian, Filipino, Indian cuisines, we have them all and and new restaurants are popping up every day! There is something for everybody from mouthwatering dim sum to spicy thai tom yum soup. Whether you're in the mood for spicy hotpot, succulent Peking duck, or flavorful dumplings, they've got it all.

But it's not just about the food. We also have wonderful specialty shops and markets. You can find everything from traditional Chinese herbs and tea to homemade vietnamese snacks and fresh-off-the-boat artworks from Asia. It's like stepping into a different world.

It is where culture comes alive, and you can truly immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere.

If you're visiting Houston for the first time, here are my top 5 restaurant recommendations:

  • Kim Son Vietnamese Buffet
  • Mala Sichuan Food
  • Crown Seafood Restaurant for dim sum
  • Tony Thai Restaurant
  • MR.Wish (grab a grapefruit tea here!)
